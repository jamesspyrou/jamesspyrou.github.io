Awards and Achievements

The Florey Philanthropic Postgraduate Travel Award (2025), the Florey Neuropix Best Scientific Image Award (2025 - published as the cover image for the 2026 Florey Annual Report), Harold Mitchell Foundation Postgraduate Travel Fellowship (2024), and the Florey Anonymous Philanthropic Postgraduate Travel Award (2023).

Australian Government Research Training Program Scholarship (Stipend and Fee Offset), an Elizabeth and Vernon Puzey Scholarship (Supplementary Stipend), and a Rowden White Scholarship, awarded by The University of Melbourne.

Norwegian Research School in Neuroscience (NRSN) and Norwegian Artificial Intelligence Research Consortium (NORA) Stipend to present at the Federation of European Neuroscience Societies (FENS) Regional Meeting 2025 and to participate in an Early Career Training Programme (ECTP) and Pre-Meeting. This ECTP involved visiting GliaLab at the Institute of Basic Medical Sciences at the University of Oslo as a host lab, and partaking in a workshop on two-photon imaging of the sleeping brain in mice.

Travel awards to present at the International Congress of Genetics (ICG) 2023, and the International Society for Stem Cell Research (ISSCR) Annual Meeting 2018.
